# Marketing / SEO articles

Search-first public articles, distinct from the in-app product help in [`/docs`](../docs).

**These are written for strangers who Googled a question** — not for existing
Coverboard users. They answer the query first, keep the hard UK-compliance detail, and
mention Coverboard once as the solution near the end. The matching `/docs` pages stay
as in-app help (they keep the feature mechanics, in-app navigation, CSV specs, and
plan-gating notes that are useful to customers but irrelevant to cold search traffic).

## Notes

- Each article carries a **"general information, not legal advice"** disclaimer —
  public compliance content aimed at strangers raises the stakes versus internal help.
- **Statutory rates are 2026/27.** When HMRC publishes new April figures, update the
  rates here as well as in the product (these are static copies, not generated).
- These are Markdown source. Wire them into whatever public surface you choose (blog
  route, GitBook public space, etc.) — they are intentionally not in the GitBook
  `SUMMARY.md`, which is product docs.
